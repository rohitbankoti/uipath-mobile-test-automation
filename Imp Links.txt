Q : Can we migrate existing Appium scripts to UiPath? 
Ans : Currently we do not have any utility available to migrate them into UiPath. However, Appium scripts can be triggered from UiPath in terms of execution.
 

Q: Can UiPath be integrated with Zephyr on Jira Cloud? 
Ans : Currently, No. It is in the road map and is expected to be available by this year-end. At the moment, integrations are available for Jira (On-Premise or Cloud) and XRAY for Jira.
